Common questions

Everything you want to know about Purple Hat.

01
Who are the courses designed for?
Our courses are designed for Degree and Diploma engineering students affiliated with universities in Maharashtra — especially students who need to cover their syllabus quickly before exams.
02
In which language are the lectures taught?
All our lectures are taught in Marathi and Hindi with English technical terms, making it very easy for Maharashtra-based students to understand complex engineering concepts.
03
How do I access the course content?
Download the Purple Hat app from Google Play or the Apple App Store. After enrolling, all course videos, notes, and question papers are available instantly on your phone.
04
What subjects are available?
We cover all major subjects across Degree Engineering (BE/B.Tech) and Diploma Engineering programs — including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, core branch subjects, and more.
05
How is Purple Hat different from other coaching institutes?
Unlike traditional institutes, we focus on exam-oriented, efficient learning. Our content is precise, our language is simple (Marathi/English), and you can study anytime on your phone — no need to attend fixed classes.
